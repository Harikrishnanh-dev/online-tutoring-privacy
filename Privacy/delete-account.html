Request Account Deletion

We’ll remove your account and associated personal data on request.

If you wish to delete your account and associated personal data, please follow one of the options below. We will verify requests to protect privacy and security before taking action.

1) Email request (recommended)

Send an email from the address registered with the account to onlinetutoring@gmail.com with the subject line Account Deletion Request. Include:

  • Your full name and the email address associated with the account
  • The reason for deletion (optional)
  • Any supporting information we can use to verify your identity

2) In-app request (if available)

If your account area contains a Delete Account function, you may use that to submit a deletion request. We will verify and process the request as described below.

What we delete

  • Account identifiers (email used to sign in) and authentication data (passwords, tokens)
  • Contact details (phone number, parent/guardian name) associated with the account
  • Enrollment records and booking data (subject to limited retention for legal or accounting reasons)
Retention note: some information (for example, transactional records required for tax or legal purposes) may be retained for a limited period even after deletion of the main account record. We will minimize retained data and scope as permitted by law.

Deletion requests are typically processed within 7 working days. We will confirm by email when deletion is complete or if we need additional information to verify the request.
