Privacy Policy

Effective date: December 23, 2025 · Online Tutoring

Introduction

Welcome. Online Tutoring ("we", "us", or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This page explains what information we may collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. We aim to be transparent and keep data handling minimal.

Information we collect

We strive for a privacy-first experience. At present:

  • We do not collect, store, or sell personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers as part of the app's normal operation.
  • We do not use tracking cookies or persistent identifiers in the app.
  • We may collect minimal technical data necessary to deliver the service (for example, crash reports or device model) only if you opt-in. Any such collection will be disclosed at the point of collection.

Log & Usage Data

We do not and will not automatically collect analytics or detailed usage logs by default. If we introduce analytics in the future, we will provide clear notice and an opt-out where required.

Third-party services

We do not use third-party services that collect user data on our behalf. If we integrate any third-party services (for example, optional payments or external hosting), we will list them here and explain what data they collect and why.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect data in our possession. While no system is 100% secure, we follow industry best practices and will notify affected users if we discover a breach that impacts personal data.

Children’s privacy

Our service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such data, please contact us and we will promptly remove it.

Your rights

You may contact us to request information about any data we hold about you, request correction or deletion, or ask questions about this policy. We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with applicable law.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. When we do, we will update the "Effective date" at the top of this page. Significant changes will be communicated where feasible.

Contact us

If you have questions or requests about your privacy, please contact us at onlinetutoring@gmail.com.
